Batect now supports shell tab completion in both [Zsh](https://www.zsh.org/) and [Fish](https://fishshell.com/).

This supports both options (eg. `./batect --out`<kbd>Tab</kbd> completes to `./batect --output`) and task names
(eg. `./batect b`<kbd>Tab</kbd> completes to `./batect build`):

To get started, update your project to Batect 0.68.0, then follow
[the installation instructions for your shell](/docs/getting-started/shell-tab-completion).
